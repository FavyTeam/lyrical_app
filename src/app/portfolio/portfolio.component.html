Ginko is the one tap contact exchange app!

Exchanging contact information has never been faster or easier. To exchange contact information, 2 or more users simply hold the ‘Ginko Me’ button and select what information they would like to share with each other. It’s that simple! Never think about updating your contacts’ information again, as the app does this automatically for you whenever there is a contact profile change.

Technologies

Android Studio, WebRTC, Google Map V3, GPS Tracking, Firebase

Ginko Me - Scan for contacts in your nearby area, exchange info with the touch of a button and have it automatically synced into your contacts.
